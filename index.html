Our Philosophy

At Breathe Botanica, we harness the natural power of terpenes to promote relaxation, clarity and ambience. Our carefully crafted products are formulated with botanical terpenes and complementary aromas to support your wellbeing. We believe in the science of nature and the importance of sustainability, which is why our products use ethically sourced ingredients and eco‑friendly packaging.

We source our terpene isolates from certified botanical suppliers. Our terpenes are 100% pure, undiluted and derived from plants like herbs and citrus fruits — containing no cannabinoids. By infusing these isolates into our wax melts, nasal inhalers and room sprays, we craft aromatic experiences tailored to your needs.

Why Terpenes?

Terpenes are aromatic compounds found across the plant kingdom. Different terpene profiles are associated with different sensory effects; for example, pinene is often described as crisp and conifer‑like, while linalool is floral and calming in aroma. We blend profiles to shape the atmosphere you want.

Quality, Safety & Compliance

  • Plant‑derived, isolated terpenes (no cannabinoids).
  • CLP/GB‑CLP aligned labelling; SDS available on request for every blend.
  • IFRA‑aware fragrance design and conservative use rates per product type.
  • Made for ambient use (melts & sprays) and brief inhalation via personal inhalers.
  • Not for ingestion, vaping or direct skin application. Keep away from children & pets.
