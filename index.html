Our Philosophy

At Breathe Botanica we harness nature’s aromatic power to promote relaxation, clarity and ambience. Every product is made in small batches using certified botanical terpenes and complementary aromas. Our isolates are 100% pure, undiluted and derived from herbs and citrus – containing no cannabinoids. Sustainability sits at the heart of our process; from eco‑friendly packaging to ethically sourced ingredients, we craft aromatic experiences you can feel good about.

Quality, Safety & Compliance

  • Plant‑derived, isolated terpenes (no cannabinoids).
  • CLP/GB‑CLP aligned labelling; SDS available on request for every blend.
  • IFRA‑aware fragrance design and conservative use rates per product type.
  • Made for ambient use (melts & sprays) and brief inhalation via personal inhalers.
  • Not for ingestion, vaping or direct skin application. Keep away from children & pets.
